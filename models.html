3D Art

I've started learning to make 3D models when I've learned of V-tubing, and wanted to give that a shot.
Sadly I was not really cut out of the streaming world, but the skills I've gained were pretty useful.
My main inspirations for the style of the models are games such as "Megaman Legends" and
"Megaman Starforce", while also taking inspiration from manga and comic books.
My main goal when making models and animaitons, is to try to capture the appeal of
2D animation and characters in a 3D environment.
